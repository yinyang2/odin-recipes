Boozy Banana Bread

Description

The best Banana Bread. Lots of Banana and a hint of almond and almond liqueur to make a delicious afternoon snack with butter and tea.

The prep time to make this is around 15 minutes, and the cook time around 1 hour. In total you can enjoy this wonderful bread in less than 2 hours. The yield of this would be around 1 bread serving a group of 8 at a time!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 5 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 drops almond extract
  • 3 drops amaretto (almond-flavored liqueur)
